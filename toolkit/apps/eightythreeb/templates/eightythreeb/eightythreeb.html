Instructions to Complete IRS 83(b) Election

  • Step 1 : Print, Review and Sign
    1. Print the document
    2. Review the IRS Transmittal letter and the two copies of your 83(b) Election.
    3. Sign in the 3 places indicated with a red cross.
    4. [if spouse sign]Have you spouse sign in 2 places indicated with a blue cross.

  • Step 2 : Upload a scan of Copy 1 to LawPal
    Upload a scanned copy of the document to LawPal. We will ensure your accountant and attorney get a copy.

  • Step 3 : Prepare an envelope containing the following
    1. The signed Transmittal Letter, Copies 1 and 2 of the 83(b) Election Letter
    2. A single self addressed, postage paid envelope for the IRS to return a date-stamped copy. Although this is not required it is recommended to request it simply to enhance the company’s and taxpayer’s records. Attach the self addressed envelope to 83(b) Election Copy 1

  • Step 4 : Mail it to the IRS
    Send it by USPS Certified Mail with Return Receipt requested to the IRS at the following address :

    [[IRS ADDRESS]]

  • Step 5 : Add the tracking number to LawPal
    Once you have sent it, input the tracking number into LawPal and we will notify all parties when the IRS have received it.
